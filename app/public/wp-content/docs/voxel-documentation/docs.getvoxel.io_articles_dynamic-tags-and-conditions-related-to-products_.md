## Dynamic tags and conditions related to products

The available [dynamic tags](docs.getvoxel.io_articles_dynamic-tags-introduction_.md) for Products

- **Minimum price:** Outputs the minimum price the product can be purchased. In some cases, this is equal to the specified Product price, however when the product consists of required addons without which a purchase cannot be made, then the minimum price output is constructed from the product price + the price of its required addons
- **Is available:** Outputs 1 or empty, depending if the product has stock or availability. The primary purpose to be used as a dynamic condition
- **Product type key**
- **Product type label**

Additional dynamic tags are available based on available product types

- **Variation images:** Available when a variable product type is being utilized. This can be used to output the variation images to a gallery or slider widget
- **Addon Label:** Outputs the label of a specific addon
- **Addon choices:** A number of dynamic tags related to the addon choices such as ID, Label, Price, Has quantity, Min quantity, Max quantity.

## Conditions

The available conditions related to Products are:

1. **[Product is available](docs.getvoxel.io_articles_product-is-available-condition_.md)**
2. **[Product type is](docs.getvoxel.io_articles_product-type-is-condition_.md)**
3. **[User has bought product](docs.getvoxel.io_articles_user-has-bought-product-condition_.md)**
